# CONTENT BENCHMARK TARGETS (AAA+)

## Objective
Đặt chuẩn nội bộ vượt mức thị trường cùng phân khúc co-op narrative action.

## Gameplay Density Targets
- New co-op interaction every 6–8 minutes average
- Unique chapter mechanic retention > 85% recognition in playtest recall
- Puzzle variation index >= 0.75 (internal metric)

## Narrative Targets
- Cutscene to gameplay ratio: 25/75
- Dialogue interruption tolerance < 3% negative feedback
- Emotional climax recall > 80% in post-playtest interviews

## Visual Targets
- Hero environment memory score > 70%
- VFX readability at 20m distance under high chaos
- Character silhouette clarity tested in 4 lighting scenarios

## Audio Targets
- Co-op success/fail sonic distinction recognition > 95%
- Voice intelligibility >= -18 LUFS integrated mix standard
- Adaptive score transition artifact rate < 1%

## QA / Stability Targets
- Crash-free session rate > 99.5%
- Network desync critical bug count = 0 at milestone lock
- Average bug reopen rate < 8%
