# TEAM & PRODUCTION PLAN (AAA SCALE)

## 1. Org Structure (peak)
- Game Direction: 1
- Design: 18
- Engineering: 40
- Art (env/char/tech art/anim/vfx): 65
- Audio (music/sfx/tech audio/voice): 14
- Narrative + Cinematic: 16
- QA + QA Automation: 35
- Production + PMO: 12
- Localization + Culturalization: 10
- Marketing integration + community lab: 8

Peak team size ≈ 219 core + external vendors.

## 2. Production Timeline (30 months)
### Phase A — Discovery (3 months)
- Finalize vision, risk register, benchmark gates

### Phase B — Pre-production (6 months)
- Vertical slice, tech validation, art bible lock

### Phase C — Production Part 1 (9 months)
- Chapters 1–3 content complete alpha

### Phase D — Production Part 2 (8 months)
- Chapters 4–6, full narrative integration, beta complete

### Phase E — Polish & Certification (4 months)
- Perf optimization, bug burn-down, platform cert, gold master

## 3. Milestone Gates
- M1: Playable prototype
- M2: Vertical slice greenlight
- M3: Alpha
- M4: Beta
- M5: Release Candidate
- M6: Gold

## 4. Quality Governance
- Weekly cross-discipline review
- Bi-weekly playtest with blind external cohort
- Monthly executive quality gate

## 5. Risk Management
- Scope creep control via chapter lock criteria
- Feature kill-list prepared each quarter
- Contingency budget for cinematic and voice re-recording
